Target Audience

The target audiences for Sun Weather are families and young adults. The layout of the page will look friendly but at the same time professional. We will choose colors and font types that will atract our audience's attention.
Families will be able to come to our website and look for activities that they can do with their families that week. We will do our best to provide all the necessary information for them to have a good time that week.
We will have spefic activities for young adults to enjoy like fitness classes, hikes, cooking classes, etc. Young adults will come and see what activities are happening and how the weather will be during the week.

Scenarios

  • I feel like doing a picnic and inviting some friends, I wonder how the weather will be this weekend?
  • What fun activities can I do with my family this weekend?
  • How will the weather be tomorrow at the place I want to go hike?
  • Will I need to bring an umbrella with me today?
  • How warm should I dress up today?
